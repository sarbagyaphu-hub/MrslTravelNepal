Nepal From Himalaya to Terai

A compact country with dramatic altitude changes, ancient Hindu and Buddhist heritage, wildlife, village life, adventure, food, festivals and heartfelt hospitality.

Himalayan Region

Above the Clouds

Everest, Annapurna, Manaslu, Kanchenjunga, Langtang, Upper Mustang and Dolpo.

  • High-altitude trekking and mountain villages
  • Sherpa culture, monasteries and snow landscapes
  • Remote expeditions and spiritual journeys
Hilly Region

Valleys, Lakes & Heritage

Kathmandu Valley, Pokhara, Bandipur, Tansen, Ilam, Gorkha and Dhulikhel.

  • Temples, traditional towns and heritage walks
  • Lakes, tea gardens and sunrise viewpoints
  • Food, festivals and village homestays
Terai Region

Wildlife & Sacred Plains

Chitwan, Lumbini, Bardia, Janakpur and the Koshi region.

  • National parks, wetlands and jungle safaris
  • Birthplace of Buddha and Mithila culture
  • Tharu villages, birdwatching and warm plains
